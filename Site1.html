    What is TreesProject ?

    The Treesproject is a project aimed at making cryptocurrencies more accessible and ecological: The Treestoken are available in limited quantities (100 Million), 80 Million are in circulation and the rest is reserved in a solidarity fund intended to plant trees and help humanitarian NGOs. When you buy Treestoken, it increases the value of this currency, each month, depending on the purchases and the fluctuation of the currency rate, part of the reserve fund will be paid to the associations and NGOs concerned below. From the moment a token is worth $0.01, each month, 0.83% of the reserve fund will be paid to its associations, so the more people buy this cryptocurrency, the more the price will increase and the more associations will collect money. money for their causes. Thus, with a redistribution rate of 0.83%/month, the Treesproject will be able to pay funds for 10 years to its beneficiary partners.
